The Populartimes - Manhattan
Welcome to the the Populartimes Manhattan!

The populartimes is a data visualization of how busy establishments get (i.e. resturants, bars, nightclubs).

Each dot represents a establishment and by using data from Google Places API, the dots will change size based on how busy the establishment is.

Click the button to find out more!

You may also click on a dot to identify the establishment.

The different colored dots represent the following establishment types:
restaurants
(red dots)
bars
(green dots)
nightclubs
(blue dots)
The size or "business" of a establishment is based off of Google Places API. The size will change depending on the specified time and date.
It should be noted that this is relatively speaking, so the dots are not always reflective of the amount of people in the area.

The dots are also selectable so that you may identify the establishment.
