The Populartimes - Manhattan
Welcome to the the Populartimes: Manhattan Edition!

The Populartimes: Manhattan Edition is a visualization of customer traffic within various establishments throughout lower/middle Manhattan during various times of the day and days of the week.

Each dot on the map represents a different establishment type
(i.e. resturants, bars, nightclubs, cafes, gyms)
and by coupling the dots with data from Google Places API, we can visually see how customer traffic at an establishment changes over time.

Click the info button to find out more about the dots!

The size or "business" of a establishment is based off of Google Places API. The size will change depending on the specified time and date.
It should be noted that this is relatively speaking, so the dots are not always reflective of the amount of people in the area.

The dots are also selectable so that you may identify the establishment.
